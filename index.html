About me

I am a final year PhD Researcher in Theoretical and Computational Neuroscience, under the supervision of Dr Cian O'Donnell, in the Intelligent Systems Research Centre, Ulster University. I did my undergraduate (MA Dubl) in Mathematics at Trinity College Dublin, then a PGCE (Mathematics) and Masters of Research (MRes) at Queen's University Belfast.

I was also a visiting PhD Researcher at the University of Bristol, in the Neural Dynamics group (School of Engineering Mathematics and Technology), under Dr Conor Houghton.

Academic Material

See here for more detail, but I am interested in (mainly recurrent) neural networks from a complex systems, neuroscience, and machine learning perspective. I am also interested in the mathematical modelling of the dynamics of synapses, neurons, and networks in their development, maturation, and computation.
